Our Story

Hi there! We're two friendly kitties named Grog (right) and Keyleth (left). Just like you, we've been leading some pretty busy lives for the last few years. Nearly every single day, we have to take care of multiple appointments like napping, staring out the window, bothering our mother, and even chasing each other at 3:00 AM--while making as much noise as possible! But, as any person on-the-go knows, you always have to make time to get your snack on. That's why we begged our mother to help us open up this cozy little snack nook for humans and kitters alike. We hope to create a space where anyone can take time out of their busy day of screaming at nothing to enjoy some treats, good headpats, chin scratches, and a quiet moment to contemplate the incomprehensible chaos of existence.
